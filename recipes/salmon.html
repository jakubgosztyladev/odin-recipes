Salmon

Description

This elegant grilled salmon features perfectly seasoned fillets with a beautiful caramelized exterior and tender, flaky interior. The salmon is delicately topped with fresh dill and aromatic herbs, creating a harmonious blend of flavors. Each fillet is cooked to perfection, retaining its natural moisture and rich, buttery texture that melts in your mouth.

Served on a bed of fresh arugula and garnished with bright lemon wedges and edible flowers, this dish is as visually stunning as it is delicious. Whether you're preparing a sophisticated dinner party or a healthy weeknight meal, this grilled salmon delivers restaurant-quality taste with the wholesome benefits of omega-3 rich fish. The combination of herbs and citrus perfectly complements the natural flavor of the salmon.

Ingredients

For the Salmon:

  • 4 salmon fillets (6 oz/170g each), skin-on
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon paprika (optional)

For Serving:

  • Fresh arugula or mixed greens
  • Lemon wedges
  • Fresh dill sprigs for garnish
  • Edible flowers (optional)
  • Extra virgin olive oil for drizzling

Steps

  1. Prepare the marinade: In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, chopped dill, parsley, oregano, salt, pepper, and paprika. Mix well to combine all the flavors.
  2. Marinate the salmon: Pat the salmon fillets dry with paper towels. Place them in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them, making sure each fillet is well coated. Cover and refrigerate for 15-30 minutes. Don't marinate longer than 30 minutes as the acid from lemon can start to "cook" the fish.
  3. Preheat your cooking surface: Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat. Alternatively, preheat your outdoor grill to medium-high (about 400째F/200째C). Lightly oil the cooking surface to prevent sticking.
  4. Cook the salmon: Remove salmon from marinade and let excess drip off. Place fillets skin-side down on the hot grill or pan. Cook for 4-5 minutes without moving them, allowing a nice crust to form.
  5. Flip and finish cooking: Carefully flip the salmon fillets using a spatula. Cook for another 3-4 minutes until the salmon is cooked through but still slightly translucent in the center. The internal temperature should reach 145째F (63째C).
  6. Rest the salmon: Remove the salmon from heat and let it rest for 2-3 minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute throughout the fish.
  7. Plate and serve: Arrange fresh arugula on serving plates. Place the salmon fillets on top. Garnish with fresh dill sprigs, lemon wedges, and edible flowers if using. Drizzle with a little extra virgin olive oil and serve immediately while hot.
