Homemade Pizza

Description

This authentic homemade pizza features a hand-stretched dough base topped with rich tomato sauce, creamy mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves. The pizza is adorned with tender mozzarella balls that melt beautifully during baking, creating pockets of gooey, stringy cheese throughout. Fresh cherry tomatoes add bursts of sweetness and acidity, perfectly balancing the richness of the cheese.

Made from scratch with simple, quality ingredients, this classic Margherita-style pizza brings the taste of Italy right to your kitchen. Whether you're hosting a pizza night with friends or enjoying a cozy dinner at home, this homemade creation delivers that perfect combination of crispy crust, flavorful toppings, and authentic Italian taste that beats any takeout.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 3 cups (375g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup (240ml) warm water (110째F/43째C)
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

For the Toppings:

  • 1 cup (240ml) tomato sauce or pizza sauce
  • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 8-10 small fresh mozzarella balls (bocconcini)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: garlic powder, red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough: In a small bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy. In a large bowl, mix flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Knead for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  2. Let the dough rise: Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.
  3. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 475째F (245째C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up. Otherwise, prepare a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  4. Shape the dough: Punch down the risen dough and transfer it to a floured surface. Roll or stretch it into a 12-inch circle, creating a slightly thicker edge for the crust.
  5. Add the sauce and toppings: Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese, then distribute the mozzarella balls and cherry tomato halves. Tear fresh basil leaves and scatter them on top.
  6. Season and drizzle: Sprinkle dried oregano, salt, and pepper over the pizza. Drizzle with olive oil for extra flavor and a golden finish.
  7. Bake the pizza: Transfer the pizza to the preheated oven (on the stone or baking sheet). Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  8. Serve: Remove from the oven, let it cool for 2-3 minutes, then slice and serve immediately. Add extra fresh basil if desired and enjoy your homemade pizza!
